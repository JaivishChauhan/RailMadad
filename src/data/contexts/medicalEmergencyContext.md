# Medical Emergency Context for Gemini

## Immediate Response Protocol
- Medical emergencies require IMMEDIATE attention
- Escalate to Railway Protection Force (RPF) and medical staff
- Provide clear instructions for emergency contacts

## Common Medical Scenarios on Trains
- Heart attacks, strokes, breathing difficulties
- Injuries from falls or accidents
- Pregnancy-related emergencies
- Diabetic emergencies
- Allergic reactions

## Emergency Contacts
- Railway Helpline: 139
- RPF Emergency: 182
- Medical Emergency: 108

## Response Guidelines
- Stay calm and provide clear instructions
- Ask for exact location (coach, berth, train number)
- Guide user to seek immediate help from train staff
- Provide first aid guidance if appropriate
- Follow up with complaint logging after emergency is handled

## Key Phrases to Recognize
- "Medical emergency", "Heart attack", "Can't breathe", "Unconscious"
- "Bleeding", "Injury", "Pain", "Pregnant woman in distress"
- "Need doctor", "Medical help", "Emergency"
