# Cleanliness Context for Gemini

## Common Cleanliness Issues
- Dirty toilets and washrooms
- Unclean coaches and seats
- Garbage accumulation
- Poor maintenance of facilities
- Pest infestations

## Standard Response Time
- Minor cleaning: 2-4 hours
- Major cleaning: 24-48 hours
- Deep cleaning: 3-7 days

## Responsible Departments
- Coach cleaning: Housekeeping Department
- Station cleaning: Station Master's office
- Toilet maintenance: Mechanical Department

## Escalation Process
1. Report to Train Ticket Examiner (TTE)
2. Contact Station Master if at station
3. File complaint through RailMadad
4. Follow up with divisional office if unresolved

## Prevention Tips
- Use dustbins provided
- Report issues immediately
- Cooperate with cleaning staff
- Maintain personal hygiene
